1. Eligibility for Refunds

This fee is applied to offset the administrative and restocking expenses incurred in processing returns under these specific conditions. Additionally, we kindly bring to your attention that any associated costs with returning the item will be the responsibility of the customer. This policy ensures transparency and allows us to continue providing quality service while addressing exceptional cases that may necessitate additional considerations. Your understanding of these terms is greatly appreciated as we strive to maintain fairness and clarity in our refund procedures.

This structured process is designed to facilitate a clear and efficient refund procedure, ensuring that all requisite information is provided for a prompt and accurate evaluation of the request. Your adherence to these guidelines is appreciated as it contributes to a streamlined and transparent resolution process for all parties involved.

3. Refund Process

Upon approval of your refund request, our commitment is to initiate the refund process within a timeframe of 30 business days. This timeline begins either upon receipt of the returned product or upon confirming the eligibility for a refund, depending on the specific circumstances of the request.
